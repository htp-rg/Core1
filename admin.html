Welcome to the Admin Dashboard

Core 1 is a comprehensive system integrating five key business functions: Talent Management, which handles the entire recruitment lifecycle; Job Management, overseeing job roles and access; Client Agreement and Management, dealing with contracts and payments; Payroll Management, covering all aspects of employee compensation; and Client Management, focused on satisfaction and engagement. Essentially, Core 1 serves as a central hub for managing human resources, client relationships, and financial administration related to employees.
